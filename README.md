# CalcuLegal Legal Damage Engine

**Deterministic legal calculation methodology for Italian LegalTech.**

CalcuLegal is a privacy-preserving legal empowerment platform that transforms official parameters, structured legal reasoning and deterministic calculation workflows into accessible preliminary simulations and awareness tools.

This repository documents the **public methodology** behind CalcuLegal: legal damage estimation, risk-oriented calculators, privacy-by-design architecture, deontological safeguards and CalcuLegal Edu awareness modules.

## Core concepts

### Deterministic Legal Damage Engine

CalcuLegal calculators are designed around deterministic calculation logic, not generative AI. The platform applies structured inputs to official or publicly explainable parameters, producing an orientative estimate or risk profile.

### Official-Parameters-Based Legal Simulation

Where possible, CalcuLegal relies on official or widely recognized legal/technical references, including tables, statutory parameters, case-law-oriented concepts and established medico-legal calculation principles.

### Balthazard Standard Implementation

The repository documents the Balthazard method as a public mathematical standard for handling multiple impairments. This does not reproduce the full commercial implementation used by CalcuLegal.

### Zero-Knowledge Lead Transfer

CalcuLegal is designed around a privacy-preserving transfer model: simulations are anonymous, and personal data is shared only after explicit user consent. The public documentation describes the architecture conceptually and does not publish operational cryptographic code.

### Legal Empowerment before Legal Advice

CalcuLegal helps people, companies, schools and families understand complex legal scenarios before seeking professional evaluation.

It supports awareness. It does not replace a lawyer.
