# Radar Reputazione & Diffamazione

Radar Reputazione & Diffamazione is an additional CalcuLegal tool focused on reputational harm, defamation risk, visibility, evidence and urgency.

## Purpose

The tool is designed to help users understand:

- whether an online/offline statement may be reputationally harmful;
- whether evidence should be preserved;
- whether urgency exists;
- whether professional evaluation may be appropriate.

## Not a compensation calculator

Unlike damage calculators, Radar Reputazione may focus more on severity, evidence, visibility and response options than on monetary estimation.

## Public methodology categories

- publication channel;
- audience/visibility;
- nature of the statement;
- persistence and virality;
- identity or anonymity of author;
- presence of sensitive data;
- evidence availability;
- urgency of removal or response.

## IP protection note

This repository does not publish the full scoring model, complete category map or production interface.
