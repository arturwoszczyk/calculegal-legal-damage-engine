# Rischio Ambientale & Bonifiche

## Live tool

The live orientative calculator is available on the official CalcuLegal website:

* [Rischio Ambientale & Bonifiche](https://www.calculegal.it/calcolo-rischio-ambientale)
* [Environmental Risk Calculator](https://www.calculegal.it/en/environmental-risk-calculator)

The live calculator is designed for companies, property owners, professionals and organizations that need a preliminary, structured and orientative understanding of environmental-risk exposure before deciding whether to seek professional evaluation.

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Rischio Ambientale & Bonifiche**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment and risk-awareness tool. It helps users structure relevant facts and understand potential categories of environmental, remediation, compliance, procedural and economic exposure.

It is not a tool for certifying environmental compliance, determining contamination, replacing technical investigations, assessing remediation duties or substituting legal, environmental, engineering or administrative evaluation.

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the environmental or remediation-related scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. organize the case around site context, contamination indicators, authority involvement, remediation exposure and compliance status;
4. distinguish between environmental, economic, administrative, documentary and operational dimensions;
5. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
6. consider relevant factors such as site use, known contamination, public-authority proceedings, urgency, potential responsible parties and available documentation;
7. return an orientative result or risk profile;
8. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Input categories

The live calculator may guide the user through structured categories such as:

* type of site or property;
* current or historical site use;
* suspected or known contamination;
* affected environmental matrix, where known;
* soil, groundwater, air or waste-related indicators;
* public-authority communications or proceedings;
* remediation or safety measures already requested;
* ownership, management or operational role;
* possible responsible-party context;
* business, transaction or real-estate implications;
* urgency or operational impact;
* available technical documents;
* procedural status or previous actions.

This repository does not publish the complete questionnaire flow or the production logic used by the live platform.

## Output categories

The calculator may provide preliminary and orientative outputs such as:

* structured summary of the environmental-risk scenario;
* preliminary risk or exposure profile;
* indication of potential remediation or compliance dimensions;
* procedural-awareness notes;
* documentary checklist;
* indication of factors requiring technical or legal review;
* awareness-oriented explanation of limits and next steps.

Outputs are not intended to certify compliance, determine contamination, quantify final remediation costs, identify legal responsibility or replace professional evaluation.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, environmental advice, engineering advice, technical certification, administrative advice, a professional opinion, a compliance assessment or a guarantee of outcome.

## Legal empowerment and risk-awareness role

The purpose of this calculator is to help companies, property owners, professionals and organizations understand whether an environmental or remediation-related situation may require further professional evaluation.

It supports awareness, organization of information and preliminary risk orientation.

It does not replace:

* a lawyer;
* an environmental consultant;
* an engineer;
* a geologist;
* a remediation expert;
* a public authority;
* a technical laboratory;
* a professional assessment of contamination, compliance, remediation duties, liability or costs.

## Methodological note: environmental-risk exposure

Environmental-risk and remediation scenarios often involve multiple layers: factual site history, technical data, regulatory procedures, authority communications, contractual exposure and economic impact.

The public methodology documents the general structure of the reasoning process and the categories of information that may be relevant to preliminary risk awareness.

This repository does not publish complete internal rules, site-risk classification thresholds, remediation-cost models or case-specific legal/technical evaluation logic.
