# Rischio Sanzionatorio D.Lgs. 231/01

## Live tool

The live orientative calculator is available on the official CalcuLegal website:

* [Rischio Sanzionatorio D.Lgs. 231/01](https://www.calculegal.it/calcolo-rischio-231)
* [Legislative Decree 231 Risk Calculator](https://www.calculegal.it/en/231-calculator)

The live calculator is designed for companies, professionals and organizations that need a preliminary, structured and orientative understanding of potential legal-economic and compliance exposure under the Italian Legislative Decree 231/2001 framework.

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Rischio Sanzionatorio D.Lgs. 231/01**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment and compliance-risk awareness tool. It helps users structure relevant facts and understand potential categories of corporate liability, predicate-offence exposure, organizational-model relevance, sanctions and procedural risk.

It is not a tool for certifying compliance, determining corporate liability, assessing the adequacy of an organizational model, replacing a legal opinion or substituting compliance, forensic, audit or judicial evaluation.

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the corporate, organizational or compliance-risk scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. organize the case around entity profile, potential predicate-offence area, organizational safeguards, procedural status and sanction exposure;
4. distinguish between monetary sanctions, disqualification/interdiction-sensitive factors, reputational exposure, compliance gaps and procedural dimensions;
5. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
6. consider relevant factors such as company size, activity sector, organizational model, supervisory controls, alleged conduct, benefit/interest indicators and authority involvement;
7. return an orientative result or risk profile;
8. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Input categories

The live calculator may guide the user through structured categories such as:

* type of organization or entity;
* business sector;
* company size or organizational complexity;
* possible predicate-offence area;
* alleged conduct or risk event;
* benefit or interest indicators;
* existence of a 231 organizational model;
* date and status of model adoption;
* supervisory body or internal-control context;
* compliance procedures or policies;
* authority involvement or proceedings;
* sanctions or precautionary measures already known;
* reputational or operational impact;
* available documents;
* procedural status or previous actions.

This repository does not publish the complete questionnaire flow or the production logic used by the live platform.

## Output categories

The calculator may provide preliminary and orientative outputs such as:

* structured summary of the 231-risk scenario;
* preliminary legal-economic and compliance exposure profile;
* indication of possible sanction-sensitive dimensions;
* awareness-oriented notes on organizational safeguards;
* procedural-risk indicators;
* documentary checklist;
* explanation of limits and next steps.

Outputs are not intended to certify compliance, determine liability, calculate final sanctions, evaluate the adequacy of a 231 model or replace professional evaluation.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, compliance advice, audit advice, forensic advice, certification, a professional opinion, a judicial assessment or a guarantee of outcome.
