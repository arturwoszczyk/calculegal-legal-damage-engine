# Licenziamento Illegittimo — Business

## Live tool

The live orientative calculator is available on the official CalcuLegal website:

* [Licenziamento Illegittimo](https://www.calculegal.it/calcolo-indennita-licenziamento)
* [Unfair Dismissal Compensation Calculator](https://www.calculegal.it/en/unfair-dismissal-compensation-calculator)

The live calculator is designed for companies, employers, HR professionals and organizations that need a preliminary, structured and orientative understanding of dismissal exposure, employment-risk factors and possible legal-economic consequences before deciding whether to seek professional evaluation.

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Licenziamento Illegittimo — Business**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment and HR/legal-risk awareness tool. It helps organizations structure relevant facts and understand potential categories of dismissal exposure, compensation ranges, procedural deadlines, compliance issues and legal-economic risk.

It is not a tool for determining whether a dismissal is lawful or unlawful, certifying HR compliance, assessing evidence, selecting a litigation strategy or replacing legal, labour-law, employment-consulting or HR evaluation.

## Keywords

employment risk, dismissal exposure, compliance, HR/legal risk, unfair dismissal, unlawful dismissal, termination risk, labour law, compensation ranges, procedural deadlines, employee protection, company size, seniority, HR compliance, dismissal challenge, settlement risk, reinstatement-sensitive scenarios

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the employment-termination or HR/legal-risk scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. organize the case around employment relationship, dismissal type, timing, company context and possible protection regime;
4. distinguish between compensation exposure, procedural deadlines, reinstatement-sensitive scenarios, compliance gaps and documentary dimensions;
5. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
6. separate economic, procedural, employment-status, organizational and HR-compliance dimensions;
7. return an orientative result or risk profile;
8. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Input categories

The live calculator may guide the user through structured categories such as:

* type of employment relationship;
* dismissal date or planned termination date;
* reason given or contemplated for termination;
* employee seniority;
* company size or organizational context;
* salary or remuneration elements;
* possible protection regime;
* disciplinary, economic or organizational grounds;
* time limits for challenge or action;
* previous communications or warnings;
* settlement attempts or negotiations;
* internal HR procedures;
* documentation available;
* procedural status or previous actions.

This repository does not publish the complete questionnaire flow or the production logic used by the live platform.

## Output categories

The calculator may provide preliminary and orientative outputs such as:

* structured summary of the employment-risk scenario;
* preliminary compensation-range awareness;
* indication of relevant time-sensitive factors;
* procedural and HR/legal-risk profile;
* documentation and process checklist;
* awareness-oriented explanation of limits and next steps.

Outputs are not intended to determine lawfulness or unlawfulness, identify the final applicable remedy, calculate a binding indemnity, certify HR compliance or replace professional evaluation.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, labour-law advice, HR advice, financial advice, compliance certification, a professional opinion, a litigation assessment or a guarantee of outcome.

## Legal empowerment and HR/legal-risk awareness role

The purpose of this calculator is to help companies, employers, HR professionals and organizations understand whether an employment-termination or dismissal-risk scenario may require further professional evaluation, especially where deadlines, documentation or procedural safeguards may be relevant.

It supports awareness, organization of information and preliminary risk orientation.

It does not replace:

* a lawyer;
* a labour-law professional;
* an employment consultant;
* an HR consultant;
* a trade-union or employee-relations specialist;
* a court;
* a professional assessment of dismissal validity, evidence, deadlines, remedies, compliance, settlement options or compensation.

## Methodological note: dismissal exposure and business risk

Unfair-dismissal scenarios often involve time-sensitive elements, compensation ranges, protection regimes, documentation quality, company size, procedural safeguards and potential litigation or settlement exposure.

The public methodology documents the general structure of the reasoning process, including the distinction between factual inputs, procedural timing, compensation awareness and HR/legal-risk indicators.

This repository does not publish complete internal rules, case-specific legal classification logic, settlement strategy logic or production calculation thresholds.
