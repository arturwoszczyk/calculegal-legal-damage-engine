# Formula di Balthazard e Invalidità Concorrenti

The Balthazard method is used to combine multiple impairments without simply adding percentages arithmetically.

## Conceptual formula

For two impairments A and B:

```text
Combined = A + B × (100 - A) / 100
```

Where A is the larger impairment percentage.

## Example

If a person has:

- impairment A = 20%
- impairment B = 10%

The combined value is not 30%.

```text
20 + 10 × (100 - 20) / 100 = 28%
```

## Why it matters

The method reflects the fact that each additional impairment affects the remaining functional capacity, not the original 100%.
