# Danno Morale and Personalizzazione

This document describes the public concept of non-economic damage personalization.

## Concept

A deterministic calculator can flag factors that may increase, decrease or require professional review of a base estimate.

Examples of relevant factors:

- intensity of suffering;
- duration;
- impact on daily life;
- family consequences;
- severity of conduct;
- exceptional circumstances.

## Repository limit

This repository does not publish CalcuLegal’s proprietary weights, thresholds or scoring logic for personalization.
