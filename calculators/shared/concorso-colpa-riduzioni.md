# Concorso di Colpa and Reductions

Some damage estimates may require reductions based on contributory fault or other legal-economic limitations.

## Public concept

A simplified reduction can be represented as:

```text
Adjusted amount = Base amount × (1 - contributory fault percentage)
```

## Example

```text
Base amount: €10,000
Contributory fault: 30%
Adjusted amount: €7,000
```

## Limit

Real legal evaluation requires professional review. This simplified concept does not decide liability.
