# Danno Fisico da Responsabilità Civile

## Live tool

The live orientative calculator is available on the official CalcuLegal website:

* [Danno Fisico da Responsabilità Civile](https://www.calculegal.it/calcolo-danno-biologico)
* [Personal Injury Calculator](https://www.calculegal.it/en/personal-injury-calculator)

The live calculator is designed for end users who want to obtain a preliminary, structured and orientative simulation before deciding whether to seek professional evaluation.

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Danno Fisico da Responsabilità Civile**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment tool. It helps users structure relevant facts and understand potential categories of damage, compensation or risk in general civil-liability and personal-injury scenarios.

It is not a tool for establishing liability, proving causation, determining medical impairment or replacing legal, medico-legal or technical evaluation.

## Keywords

general civil liability, biological damage, personal injury, domestic accidents, aggression, product defects, premises liability, third-party liability, non-economic damage, permanent impairment, temporary injury, civil compensation

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the factual civil-liability scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. organize the case around event type, injury consequences, damage categories and possible responsibility context;
4. distinguish between temporary injury, permanent biological damage, economic losses and procedural elements;
5. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
6. consider relevant factors such as age, impairment values, temporary disability periods, expenses and supporting documentation;
7. return an orientative result or risk profile;
8. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Input categories

The live calculator may guide the user through structured categories such as:

* type of harmful event;
* context of the injury;
* role of the user;
* possible responsible party;
* temporary injury period;
* permanent biological damage values, if known;
* medical expenses;
* other economic consequences;
* moral or non-economic damage indicators;
* available documents;
* procedural status or previous communications.

This repository does not publish the complete questionnaire flow or the production logic used by the live platform.

## Output categories

The calculator may provide preliminary and orientative outputs such as:

* structured summary of the injury scenario;
* possible damage categories;
* preliminary compensation or risk profile;
* indication of factors affecting the simulation;
* documentary checklist;
* awareness-oriented explanation of limits and next steps.

Outputs are not intended to determine liability, prove causation, quantify a final claim or replace professional evaluation.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, medico-legal advice, medical advice, technical assessment, a professional opinion or a guarantee of outcome.

## Legal empowerment role

The purpose of this calculator is to help users understand whether their personal-injury or civil-liability situation may require further professional evaluation.

It supports awareness, organization of information and preliminary orientation.

It does not replace:

* a lawyer;
* a medico-legal expert;
* a physician;
* a technical consultant;
* a court-appointed expert;
* a professional assessment of liability, causation or damages.
