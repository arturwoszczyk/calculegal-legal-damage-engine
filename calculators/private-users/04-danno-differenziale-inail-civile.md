# Danno Differenziale INAIL / Civile

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Danno Differenziale INAIL / Civile**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment tool. It helps users structure relevant facts and understand potential categories of damage, compensation or risk.

## Keywords

INAIL compensation, civil damages, homogeneous items, employer responsibility, art. 2087 c.c.

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the factual scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
4. separate economic, non-economic and procedural dimensions;
5. return an orientative result or risk profile;
6. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, not a professional opinion and not a guarantee of outcome.
