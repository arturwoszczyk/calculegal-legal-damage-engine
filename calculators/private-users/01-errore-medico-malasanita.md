# Errore Medico & Malasanità

## Live tool

The live orientative calculator is available on the official CalcuLegal website:

* [Errore Medico & Malasanità](https://www.calculegal.it/calcolo-risarcimento-errore-medico)
* [Medical Malpractice Compensation Calculator](https://www.calculegal.it/en/medical-malpractice-compensation-calculator)

The live calculator is designed for end users who want to obtain a preliminary, structured and orientative simulation before deciding whether to seek professional evaluation.

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Errore Medico & Malasanità**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment tool. It helps users structure relevant facts and understand potential categories of damage, compensation or risk in healthcare-liability scenarios.

It is not a tool for diagnosing medical conditions, establishing professional liability, proving causation or replacing medico-legal or legal evaluation.

## Keywords

medical malpractice, healthcare liability, informed consent, loss of chance, biological damage, medico-legal assessment, healthcare damage, hospital liability, medical negligence, patient rights

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the factual healthcare scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. organize the case around medical event, damage type, timeline and potential responsibility area;
4. distinguish between known and unknown medico-legal impairment values where applicable;
5. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
6. separate biological, moral, economic, procedural and documentary dimensions;
7. return an orientative result or risk profile;
8. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Input categories

The live calculator may guide the user through structured categories such as:

* type of healthcare event;
* medical area or treatment context;
* alleged error, omission, delay or complication;
* timing of the event and discovery of damage;
* known or unknown medico-legal impairment values;
* temporary and permanent consequences;
* economic expenses or losses;
* loss of chance or aggravation scenarios;
* informed consent issues;
* available documents;
* procedural status or previous actions.

This repository does not publish the complete questionnaire flow or the production logic used by the live platform.

## Output categories

The calculator may provide preliminary and orientative outputs such as:

* structured summary of the scenario;
* possible damage categories;
* preliminary damage or risk profile;
* documentary checklist;
* indication of factors requiring professional review;
* awareness-oriented explanation of limits and next steps.

Outputs are not intended to determine liability, quantify a final claim or replace expert evaluation.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, medico-legal advice, medical advice, a professional opinion, a diagnosis, a liability assessment or a guarantee of outcome.

## Legal empowerment role

The purpose of this calculator is to help users understand whether their situation may require further professional evaluation.

It supports awareness, organization of information and preliminary orientation.

It does not replace:

* a lawyer;
* a medico-legal expert;
* a physician;
* a court-appointed expert;
* a professional assessment of causation, liability or damages.
