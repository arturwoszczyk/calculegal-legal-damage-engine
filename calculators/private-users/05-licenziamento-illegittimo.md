# Licenziamento Illegittimo

## Live tool

The live orientative calculator is available on the official CalcuLegal website:

* [Licenziamento Illegittimo](https://www.calculegal.it/calcolo-indennita-licenziamento)
* [Unfair Dismissal Compensation Calculator](https://www.calculegal.it/en/unfair-dismissal-compensation-calculator)

The live calculator is designed for end users who want to obtain a preliminary, structured and orientative simulation before deciding whether to seek professional evaluation.

## Purpose

This document describes the public methodology behind the CalcuLegal calculator for **Licenziamento Illegittimo**.

The calculator is designed as a preliminary legal empowerment tool. It helps users structure relevant facts and understand potential compensation ranges, time limits, procedural risks and legal-economic exposure in employment-termination scenarios.

It is not a tool for determining whether a dismissal is unlawful, identifying the correct judicial remedy, assessing evidence or replacing labour-law evaluation.

## Keywords

employment termination, unfair dismissal, unlawful dismissal, time limits, compensation ranges, employee protection, labour law, dismissal challenge, reinstatement, indemnity, seniority, company size, employment contract, procedural deadlines

## Public methodology

The calculator follows a structured pattern:

1. identify the employment-termination scenario;
2. collect legally relevant non-identifying inputs;
3. organize the case around employment relationship, dismissal type, timing, company context and possible protection regime;
4. distinguish between compensation ranges, procedural deadlines, reinstatement-sensitive scenarios and legal-risk factors;
5. apply deterministic reasoning or official/public parameters where appropriate;
6. separate economic, procedural, employment-status and documentary dimensions;
7. return an orientative result or risk profile;
8. remind the user that professional evaluation remains necessary.

## Input categories

The live calculator may guide the user through structured categories such as:

* type of employment relationship;
* dismissal date or communication date;
* reason given for termination;
* seniority;
* company size or organizational context;
* salary or remuneration elements;
* possible protection regime;
* disciplinary or economic grounds;
* time limits for challenge or action;
* previous communications or settlement attempts;
* available documents;
* procedural status or previous actions.

This repository does not publish the complete questionnaire flow or the production logic used by the live platform.

## Output categories

The calculator may provide preliminary and orientative outputs such as:

* structured summary of the employment-termination scenario;
* preliminary compensation-range awareness;
* indication of relevant time-sensitive factors;
* procedural-risk profile;
* documentary checklist;
* awareness-oriented explanation of limits and next steps.

Outputs are not intended to determine unlawfulness, identify the final applicable remedy, calculate a binding indemnity or replace professional evaluation.

## Output nature

Outputs are preliminary, orientative and non-binding.

They are not legal advice, labour-law advice, financial advice, a professional opinion, a litigation assessment or a guarantee of outcome.

## Legal empowerment role

The purpose of this calculator is to help users understand whether their employment-termination situation may require further professional evaluation, especially where deadlines or procedural steps may be relevant.

It supports awareness, organization of information and preliminary orientation.

It does not replace:

* a lawyer;
* a labour-law professional;
* a trade-union adviser;
* an employment consultant;
* a court;
* a professional assessment of dismissal validity, evidence, deadlines, remedies or compensation.

## Methodological note: time limits and compensation ranges

Unfair-dismissal scenarios often involve time-sensitive elements and compensation ranges that depend on multiple legal and factual variables.

The public methodology documents the general structure of the reasoning process, including the distinction between factual inputs, procedural timing and orientative compensation awareness.

This repository does not publish complete internal rules, case-specific legal classification logic or production calculation thresholds.
