# CalcuLegal Edu Roadmap

Planned or potential modules:

- Cyberbullismo
- Stalking Condominiale
- Mobbing & Lavoro
- Maltrattamenti Minori
- Violenza Ostetrica
- Violenza Domestica
- Truffe & Identità
- Discriminazione

Each module should follow the CalcuLegal Edu principles:

- prevention;
- awareness;
- early orientation;
- institutional resources;
- no legal advice;
- minimal data collection;
- safety-first interface.
