# Palestra Educativa

Palestra Educativa is the preventive and educational side of CalcuLegal Edu.

## Components

- myths and false beliefs;
- scenario-based choices;
- consequence visualization;
- empathy exercises;
- guided discussion.

## Educational goals

- make legal and emotional consequences visible;
- create classroom discussion;
- help parents understand digital dynamics;
- encourage early intervention;
- reduce minimization of harmful conduct.

## IP protection note

This repository does not contain the full scenario database or complete scoring logic.
