# Pronto Soccorso Digitale

Pronto Soccorso Digitale is a safety-first orientation pattern for digital harm.

## Purpose

Help users quickly identify:

- whether a situation may be urgent;
- whether evidence should be preserved;
- whether trusted adults or authorities should be contacted;
- which institutional resources may be relevant.

## Examples of resource categories

- emergency services;
- child support services;
- postal/cyber police;
- privacy authority;
- school contacts;
- psychological support.

## Important disclaimer

This module does not replace emergency services, law enforcement, school safeguarding procedures, psychological support or professional legal advice.
