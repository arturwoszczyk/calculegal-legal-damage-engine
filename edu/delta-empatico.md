# Delta Empatico

Delta Empatico is a CalcuLegal Edu concept designed to reveal perception gaps between adults and minors in digital harm situations.

## Concept

Cyberbullying is not only a legal issue. It often involves a gap between:

- how adults think a situation is perceived;
- how minors actually experience the situation;
- what each side believes the other side thinks.

## Educational use

A module may ask separate participants to rate:

- perceived objective gravity;
- expected suffering;
- guessed perception of the other participant.

The goal is not diagnosis. The goal is conversation.

## Public pseudologic

```text
adult_self_score
adult_guess_child_score
child_self_score
child_guess_adult_score

empathy_gap = abs(adult_guess_child_score - child_self_score)
meta_gap = abs(child_guess_adult_score - adult_self_score)
```
