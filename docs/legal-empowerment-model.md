# Legal Empowerment Model

CalcuLegal follows the principle:

> Legal Empowerment before Legal Advice.

The platform helps people understand complex legal scenarios before they decide whether to consult a professional.

## Purpose

The purpose is to reduce asymmetry between people and legal systems by providing:

- accessible explanations;
- structured questions;
- orientative estimates;
- risk-awareness tools;
- privacy-preserving pathways;
- institutional resources where appropriate.

## Not a substitute for lawyers

Legal empowerment is not legal advice. It does not replace the role of lawyers, courts, public authorities or qualified professionals.

It helps users arrive better prepared, with clearer facts and questions.

## User benefit

A user can:

- understand possible categories of damage or risk;
- identify missing information;
- recognize urgency;
- decide whether to seek help;
- avoid sending unnecessary personal data at the first stage.

## Professional benefit

A professional may receive better-structured requests, where users have already reflected on key facts and documents.

This repository does not publish the operational professional-transfer workflow.
