# No Ranking, No Marketplace, No Intermediation

CalcuLegal is not designed as a lawyer ranking system or a legal marketplace.

## No ranking

The platform does not rank lawyers, certify the best lawyer, compare professionals or assign scores to law firms.

## No outcome promise

The platform does not promise, guarantee or imply a legal result.

## No interference with professional independence

Any professional evaluation remains autonomous. CalcuLegal does not control legal strategy, professional judgment, fees, litigation decisions or client-lawyer relationship.

## Optional user-controlled transfer

Where a user chooses to request professional orientation, the transfer is based on explicit consent and user choice. This repository does not include the production routing logic.
