# No Legal Advice

CalcuLegal is designed as an informational and orientative platform.

It does not provide legal advice, legal opinions, litigation strategy, procedural instructions or professional representation.

## What the platform can do

- explain categories of damage or risk;
- structure user-provided facts;
- apply public or explainable calculation principles;
- return preliminary and non-binding simulations;
- suggest that professional evaluation may be useful.

## What the platform cannot do

- determine whether a claim will succeed;
- evaluate the full evidentiary record;
- replace a lawyer;
- replace a medico-legal expert;
- draft legal acts;
- create a professional relationship;
- guarantee compensation;
- certify legal risk.

## Professional review remains necessary

Any real case should be reviewed by a qualified professional before action is taken.
